How To Use Lyrimix

What is Lyrimix For?

Lyrimix is built as a convenient and customizable way to easily visualize lyrics and other forms of expression/speech. Lyrimix let's you customize everything from the theme, the timing, the color, the words, and the animations. Bring your vision to life with each key you press!

Different Modes

Line Mode: In Line Mode multiple words can appear on the screen at once. This way, you can control whether single words, one line, or entire verses appear at once!

↑

↓

shift

shows next word on new line (emphasized color)

Single Word Only Mode: Single Word Only Mode is exactly as it sounds! This mode is far more manageable. Instead of worrying about linebreaks, all you have to control when the next word appears!
